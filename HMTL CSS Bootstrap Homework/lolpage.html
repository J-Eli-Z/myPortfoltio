Yasuo

Yasuo, the stupidly op, but yet, could still be callled one of the worst and yet good at the same time champion in League of Legends, is my favorite hero. Nothing could go wrong with playing Yasuo. Either you go 10 0 or you wait for the 0 10 power spike. The most absurd thing is his wind wall. Isn't it just funny you could block a rocket with a wind... wall. And he's damage is no joke. He looks like he's performing some simple cut and stab, but actually you just can't see his profound stab and cut. It's literally like the ones in manhwas, "For a thousand years, he stabbed and cut". As like Yasuo has said "No cure for fools", there's also no cure for his absurdness.
